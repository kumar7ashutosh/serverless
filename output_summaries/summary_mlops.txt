MLOps (Machine Learning Operations) is an emerging field that combines Machine Learning, DevOps, and Data Engineering to bridge the gap between ML experimentation and real-world applications. MLOps aims to bring agility and reliability to ML development by emphasizing automation, collaboration, and iterative processes. Key aspects of MLOps include:

1. Automation of repetitive tasks
2. Version control for reproducibility and rollback
3. Continuous Integration/Continuous Delivery (CI/CD) for seamless updates
4. Monitoring and logging for real-time +performance tracking
5. Collaboration between data scientists, engineers, and operations teams

By adopting MLOps practices, organizations can accelerate the deployment of ML solutions, improve their reliability, and unlock the true potential of artificial intelligence, ultimately transforming promising ML experiments into impactful and sustainable business value.
